Welcome to Asphatl Rejends!

The ultimate high-speed racing experience! Get ready to dive into the thrilling world of street racing, where you can challenge yourself and others on the most exciting tracks. Take all the bets, outspeed your opponent, and finish it with a style!

How to Play?

Choose your favourite car, pick your most suitable map, and smoke everyone with your skills! Brake, drift, and boost. You can also out-trick your opponent to slow them down by crashing onto them! Use your nitro-boost to improve your odds at facing your opponent, or to win the race!

Join the Conversation

Connect with fellow racers, share your experiences, and stay updated with upcoming interesting events and updates. Each week, we hold a new event to keep things fresh and heats up for your car to race! The more, the merrier!
